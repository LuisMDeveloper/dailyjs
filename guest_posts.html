Writing Guest Posts for DailyJS

You can contribute guest posts about any JavaScript-related topic. Suitable content includes:

  • Tutorials for techniques and libraries
  • Coverage of a new library, API or application
  • Book reviews

The style guide is in the site's README.

We prefer text submitted in textile. See the DailyJS source to figure out how we do formatting.

You can include a short bio about yourself with a link to your site.
