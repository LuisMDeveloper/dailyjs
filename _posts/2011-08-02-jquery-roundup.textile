h3. Timeago

!/images/posts/timeago.png!

"Timeago":http://timeago.yarp.com/ (GitHub: "rmm5t / jquery-timeago":https://github.com/rmm5t/jquery-timeago, License: _MIT_) by Ryan McGeary generates fuzzy timestamps (like "less than a minute ago") from ISO 8601 formatted dates and times:

{% highlight javascript %}
$('selector').timeago();
{% endhighlight %}

It even has i18n support!

This plugin takes advantage of the "Datetime Design Pattern":http://microformats.org/wiki/datetime-design-pattern microformat.

h3. glDatePicker

!/images/posts/gldatepicker.png!

"glDatePicker":http://code.gautamlad.com/glDatePicker/ (GitHub: "glad / glDatePicker":https://github.com/glad/glDatePicker/) by Gautam Lad is a lightweight date picker, featuring:

* Forward/backward navigation
* Current date highlight
* Various date selection and navigation restriction options
* Styles per-date picker

The most basic usage is just <code>$('#date1').glDatePicker()</code>, and there are lots of examples on the project's homepage.
